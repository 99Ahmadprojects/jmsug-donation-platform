Data Deletion Instructions

Jamia Masjid Sayyidina Usman Ghani (JMSUG) respects your privacy. If you have submitted a donation proof via our portal and wish to have your personal data removed from our systems, you have the right to request its deletion.

How to request Data Deletion:

To have your data deleted, please follow these simple steps:

  1. Send a message from the WhatsApp number you used to submit the form.
  2. Message our official number: 0316 5234606
  3. Include the text: "Please delete my data from the JMSUG database. My name is [Your Name]."

What happens next?

Upon receiving your request, our administration will manually process it within 3-5 business days. We will permanently delete the following information associated with your number:

  • Your submitted Name
  • Your Phone/WhatsApp Number
  • The payment proof screenshot associated with your submission

Note: We may retain a non-identifiable, anonymous record of the donation amount and category for legal accounting purposes, but it will no longer be attached to your personal information.
