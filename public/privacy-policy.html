Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Welcome to the official donation portal for Jamia Masjid Sayyidina Usman Ghani (JMSUG). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a donation proof through our portal, we collect the following personal information:

  • Personal Details: Your full name.
  • Contact Information: Your WhatsApp/Phone number.
  • Donation Details: The category of your donation (e.g., Zakat, Fitra) and the uploaded screenshot of your payment proof.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To verify and process your donation to the Masjid.
  • To maintain an internal record of donations for accounting and transparency.
  • To send you automated confirmation and verification messages via WhatsApp using the WhatsApp Business API.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone. However, we use secure third-party services to operate our portal:

  • Meta Platforms (WhatsApp API): We share your phone number and name strictly for the purpose of sending you WhatsApp notifications regarding your donation status.
  • Cloudinary: We use Cloudinary to securely host and store the payment proof images you upload.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. Your data is stored securely in our database, and access is restricted to authorized Masjid administrators only.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, please contact us via WhatsApp at 0316 5234606.
